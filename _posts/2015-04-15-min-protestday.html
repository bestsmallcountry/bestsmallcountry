Protest Day outside Minister Alan Kelly's constituency office


Thank you Nenagh

Have to say, the people of Nenagh were most hospitable, and the first thing that happened was a lady went and bought me a coffee. People came up and told me their frustrations with the Minister honked their car horns and otherwise expressed support.

The obvious lingering question is: "How did he get reelected?"
