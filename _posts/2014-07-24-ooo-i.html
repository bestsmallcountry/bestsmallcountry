Dear Mr McWeeney

I refer to previous correspondence regarding your appeal of the {% include m/office_of_the_ombudsman.span content="Ombudsman" %} decision concerning your complaint against {% include m/longford_county_council.span content="Longford County Council." %} You lodged your appeal with this Office on 4 March 2014. I wrote to you on 10 March 2014 inviting you to provide additional information in support of your appeal. At your request I suspended consideration of your appeal on 7 April 2014 to allow you additional time to provide further information in support of your appeal. You were seeking to obtain a copy of the Council's report to this Office.

I understand that your separate requests, under FOI and {% include m/data_protection.span content="Data Protection" %} legislation for a copy of the Council's report have both been refused by this Office. I also understand that you are considering appealing these decisions to the Office of the Information Commissioner and the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner respectively. I wish to inform you that it will not be possible to suspend your appeal indefinitely pending the outcome of your appeals to these offices.

I must now inform you that I intend to proceed with your appeal and to assign it to a Senior Investigator who was not previously involved in the examination of your complaint. If you wish to provide any further additional information in support of your appeal you should do so by 1 August 2014.
