Dear Mr. McWeeney,

{% include m/andrew_patterson.span content="I" %} refer to your letter which we received on 28 March 2014 in which you request under the Data Protection Act, 1988 and 2003 a copy of the detailed report sent to your Offices by the council/s concerning case number L30/13/1772.

With regard to file L30/13/1772, section 9 (1) of the Ombudsman Act, 1980 provides that information provided by the {% include m/peter_tyndall.span content="Ombudsman" %} in the course of a preliminary examination or an investigation under the Act shall not be disclosed except for the purposes envisaged in Subsections (1)(a) or (1)(b). I enclose a copy of this section for your information. As far as your request is concerned, the Data Protection Act, 1988 (Restriction of Section 4) Regulations 1989 (S.I. 81 of 1989) provide that the right of access conferred by Section 4 of the Data Protection Act, 1988 does not apply to Section 9 of the Ombudsman Act, 1980. A copy of these regulations is also enclosed.

Since Section 4 of the Data Protection Act, 1988 does not apply to this data and since the disclosure which you seek is precluded by the terms of the Ombudsman Act, I regret that I can not accede to your request.
