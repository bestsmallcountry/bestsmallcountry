19/07/2012

Dear Mr. McWeeney,

I am writing to you in relation to a number of complaints that have been brought to my attention pertaining to you while using the facilities at Longford Sports & Leisure Centre.

These complaints relate to alleged incidents in the pool over the past three months. {% include m/david_sheridan.span content="David Sheridan" %}, Assistant Manager has spoken to you and made you aware that there has been one verbal and two written complaints made about your behaviour in the swimming pool.

The complaints specifically relate to incidents of bullying and verbal abuse.

These are considered very serious matters and we do not condone any such acts at Longford Sports & Leisure Centre.

I trust you understand the severity of these complaints and the necessity to make you aware of these.

We, at Longford Sports & Leisure Centre, pride ourselves on providing a service to our customers that ensures each one has an enjoyable and safe visit to our facility.

The rules and regulations of the facility are in place to help ensure this and every customer is expected to abide by these.

As a regular customer at Longford Sports & Leisure centre, I am sure you understand that people use our facility for various reasons e.g. recreational, fitness, therapy etc. Each and every user is entitled to enjoy their visit without feeling under any threat from any other user.

You are a valued customer to our facility but if we receive any more complaints to this nature we will be forced to refuse you entry to our facility.

We trust, with your co-operation with the centres rules and staff, we can continue to serve your leisure needs.


Yours sincerely
Mr. Dan Rooney
Secretary
