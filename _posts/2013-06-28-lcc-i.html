{% include m/mary_morrissey.span content="I" %} refer to your recent your Freedom of Information request seeking records that may be held by Longford Sports and Leisure Ltd. in relation to policies and procedures.

My decision is to part-grant your request in accordance with the provisions of Freedom of Information legislation.

I enclose copies of available written records:

  1. A copy of the Data Protection Policy; the Access Policy in the form of the Terms and Conditions of Membership (which refers to biometrics) and a copy of the Pool Rules. Such policies were in place in 2012
  2. Copies of personal correspondence; company correspondence to you and copies of your membership forms

I find that I must refuse access to the Complaints Response / Disciplinary Procedures in accordance with Section 10 of the Freedom of Information Acts 1997 - 2003 which states: a head to whom a request under section 7 is made may refuse to grant the request if (a) the record concerned does not exist or cannot be found after all reasonable steps to ascertain its whereabouts have been taken .

If you are not satisfied with the decision on your request you may apply in writing within 4 weeks (ie: 20 working days) to the Senior Executive Officer, Human Resources, Longford County Council, Gt. Water St., Longford for an internal review.
