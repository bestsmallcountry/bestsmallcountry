I refer to your recent email to this Office.

A very small number of agencies are not required to deal with access requests under the Data Protection Acts. The {% include m/peter_tyndall.span content="Office of the Ombudsman" %} is one of those organisations. While we do not have sight of the response that you have received from The Office of the Ombudsman, there is a legal basis which provides that they do not have to provide access to information which they have {% include m/longford_county_council.span content="obtained" %} in the course of an {% include m/danny_smith.span content="investigation" %} {% include m/aidan_moore.span content="or" %} {% include m/andrew_patterson.span content="examination." %}

Accordingly it would appear that {% include m/data_protection.span content="this Office" %} is not in a position to investigate this matter on your behalf.
